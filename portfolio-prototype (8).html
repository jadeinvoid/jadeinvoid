Where the terminal
meets the brush.

Based in Canada, rooted in Seoul. I design terminal interfaces and illustration systems that carry the clarity of Korean craft — purposeful, minimal, with meaning in every element.

My work applies TUI philosophy beyond the terminal: strict grid, deliberate whitespace, nothing superfluous. The obangsaek palette grounds everything I make in a colour language older than screens.
